My Biography:

Journey's Inception...
My self Harsh Patel, born and raised in village named Vatadra which is located in the central part of Gujarat in India. Later, at the age of 6, I was admitted to the school which was about 15kms away from my village. My school name was Supath E.M.H.S located near the Gulf of Cambay. I studied their for 14 years, from Playgroup to 12th grade. I use stay in top 5 rankings every year of my schooling.

Schooling Period...
There were ample of Co-Curricular activites including Sports, Inter-school Tournaments and many more held in my school. I was very fond of it, so I use to participate in each and every activaties, every year. During first 8 to 9 years I didn't recieved anything special in my achievements. But, as a grew up and started understanding the things I started participating in more activities and here the game begins... I was in 10th grade and I recieved the Hall of Fame For which I was working since my childhood. In that year we had a Science Fair, and I was leading my group, we decided to built a Robotic Remote Control Car. Also, there was a Grand Sports Tournament so, I participated in that too. And lastly the day I was waiting for that came it was Annual Award Function Day. Supraisingly, my father was called as the Chief Guest of the event. I was not ready to belive that he is here and my work was done.

Achievements...
On that day I was holding the title of: "student of the year", "Sports Championship", "Best Project of the Science Fair", "Best Student Teachers". And during my 14 years of schooling I recieved about 89 Certificates. Hence, this were the achievements I attained and I was really very happy to see my father on stage and making them proud to be my parent.

Goals...
Afterall, before entering to the important years of my schooling i.e 11th and 12th grade, I decided to pursue my further studies in Canada after completing my 12th Grade. And, I made it scuccessfully.... I completed my 12th grade with 81%, later, I started preparing of IELTS to move 1 step ahead towards my goals. And again I cracked it. And finally I am here to complete my further studies..

To summarize "Experiencing a scuccessfull Life" That I made it happen, I succed in each and every step of reaching my goals. Although many barriers came but I tackled and this experience defines me that also in future, I am not going to stop untill and unless I will achieve all my goals and change the status of "Experiencing a scuccessfull life" to "Experienced a scuccessfull life".
